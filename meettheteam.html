Board of Directors


Adelyn Chen

President

Adelyn Chen is a junior at Design Tech High School, and the President of the TEDxDesignTechHighSchool club. She loves to play the violin and is the co-concertmaster of Peninsula Youth Orchestra. In addition, Adelyn is Co-President of Design Tech's Leadership Club. In her spare time, she can be found writing calligraphy.



Isabelle Yu

Vice President

Isabelle Yu is a junior at Design Tech High School, and the Vice President of TEDxDesignTechHighSchool. She participates in Leadership and Yearbook as well and is passionate about journalism. In her spare time she enjoys listening to music, drinking boba and taking photos.



Benjamin Chang

Curator

Benjamin Chang is an 11th grade student at d.tech and is a Curator for the TEDxDesignTechHighSchool club. He enjoys jazz, photography and video games and hopes to become a astronomer or biologist in the future.



Brandon Liu

Curator

Brandon Liu is a junior at Design Tech High School and a Curator for TEDxDesignTechHighSchool. He spends most of his time on school work and extracurriculars. The rest of his time is devoted to his wonderful dog.

Jane Wang

Lead Designer

Jane Wang is a junior at d.tech and Lead Designer for TEDxDesignTechHighSchool. She has a passion for art and books, as well as human and cultural rights. At home, you’ll probably find her eating almond butter oatmeal.



Vani Suresh
